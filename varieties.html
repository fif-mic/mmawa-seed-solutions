Secondary Sourcing Tier

Additional Varieties & International Genetics

Beyond our core Kutsaga-based portfolio, supplementary approved international genetics may be added to M'mawa's variety offering where regulator-approved and agronomically justified.

This secondary sourcing tier enables M'mawa Seed Solutions to broaden its portfolio in response to evolving market requirements, farmer demand, and agronomic conditions — while maintaining the same rigorous standards of traceability, certification, and quality assurance that define our core Zimbabwe-origin supply.

Any additions to the portfolio will be subject to full regulatory approval, applicable certification requirements, and batch-level traceability before being made available to farmers.
