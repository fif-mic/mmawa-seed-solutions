Verify Before You Plant

Check your seed before it enters the seedbed.

Trust should not be a guess. Farmers should be able to check the pack, the batch, the seller, and the source before planting.

Warning signs of fake seed

  • No batch number or lot number
  • Packaging looks resealed or unofficial
  • Seller is unknown or not recommended
  • Price is suspiciously low
  • No source or certification information
  • Variety name cannot be verified
